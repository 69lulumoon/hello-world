This X Does Not Exist
This X Does Not Exist is a website that links to numerous other websites that will generate images of things, humans or animals that do not exist. This website is noteworthy for its use of unsophisticated Ai image generation as the websites all utilize outdated AI technology. In a sense it is a time capsule that allows the visitor to look back at a time where AI was still new and rough around the edges.
